### Summary 摘要

The paper proposes Directionally Aligned Federated Aggregation (DAFA), a server-side aggregation rule that assigns softmax weights to client updates based on their cosine similarity with a proxy global descent direction estimated via momentum on the server. The authors introduce a Directional Signal-to-Noise Ratio (DSNR) as a lens for analyzing aggregated updates, derive a surrogate lower bound that motivates alignment-based weighting, provide bounds on aggregation bias tied to the softmax temperature, and state non-convex convergence guarantees with an improved heterogeneity constant over FedAvg. Empirically, DAFA shows sizable gains over standard baselines (FedAvg, FedProx, SCAFFOLD, FedNova) on CIFAR-10, FEMNIST, and Shakespeare under heterogeneous settings, with ablations on the temperature and proxy momentum.
论文提出了方向对齐联合聚合（DAFA），这是一种服务器端聚合规则，根据客户端更新与通过动量估计的代理全局下降方向的余弦相似度，为客户端更新分配软最大权重。作者引入了方向信噪比（DSNR）作为分析聚合更新的视角，推导出一个替代下界以激励基于比对的加权，提供了与软最大温度相关的聚合偏差界限，并陈述了非凸收敛保证，且在 FedAvg 上具有改进的异质性常数。实证上，DAFA 在异质条件下，在 CIFAR-10、FEMNIST 和 Shakespeare 上，在温度和代理动量的消融条件下，相较标准基线（FedAvg、FedProx、SCAFFOLD、FedNova）有显著提升。



### Strengths 优势

- Technical novelty and innovation

  
  技术创新与创新

  - Introduces a directional perspective on federated aggregation via DSNR and proposes a simple, scalable alignment-based weighting that is purely server-side.
    引入了通过 DSNR 进行联邦聚合的定向视角，并提出了一种简单、可扩展的基于对齐的权重，纯粹是服务器端的。
  - The softmax-over-cosine idea with a momentum-based proxy is elegant and computationally light, and the DSNR framing provides an intuitive unifying view of “signal-vs-noise” in heterogeneous FL.
    基于动量的代理的软极大余弦概念优雅且计算量轻，DSNR 框架提供了异构 FL 中“信号与噪声”的直观统一视图。

- Experimental rigor and validation

  
  实验严谨性与验证

  - Evaluations span three standard FL benchmarks with heterogeneous partitions and include multiple baselines (FedAvg, FedProx, SCAFFOLD, FedNova).
    评估涵盖三个标准 FL 基准，具有异构划分，并包含多个基线（FedAvg、FedProx、SCAFFOLD、FedNova）。
  - Ablation studies on key hyperparameters (temperature γ and momentum β) and empirical assessment of proxy reliability provide insight into the mechanism.
    对关键超参数（温度γ和动量β）的消融研究以及代理可靠性的实证评估为其机制提供了洞见。
  - Empirical DSNR and variance analyses support the directional alignment intuition and correlate with performance.
    经验性 DSNR 和方差分析支持方向对齐直觉，并与性能相关。

- Clarity of presentation

   表达清晰

  - The motivation, algorithm, and high-level theory are presented accessibly with a clear algorithm box and sensible assumptions.
    动机、算法和高层理论通过清晰的算法框和合理的假设，易于理解地呈现。
  - The connection between DSNR, directional alignment, and softmax weights is well-motivated conceptually.
    DSNR、方向对准和软最大权重之间的联系在概念上是有充分动机的。

- Significance of contributions

  
  贡献的重要性

  - Addresses an important pain point in FL—instability and inefficiency under heterogeneity—using a simple mechanism that is broadly deployable (no extra client state/communication).
    解决了 FL 中一个重要的痛点——异构性下的不稳定性和低效——采用一种简单且可广泛部署的机制（无需额外的客户端状态/通信）。
  - Demonstrates robust empirical improvements, especially under severe non-IID, suggesting practical impact.
    在严重非 IID 下，显示出强劲的实证改进，显示出实际效果。

### Weaknesses 弱点

- Technical limitations or concerns

  
  技术限制或担忧

  - Theorem 1’s DSNR surrogate bound appears to ignore the deterministic drift term in the denominator Eε[||Δagg − Δ*||^2]; with Δi = Δ* + di + εi and softmax weights summing to 1, this denominator equals ||∑i wi di||^2 + Σε w_i^2 Var(εi) and thus generally includes a drift contribution that depends on weights. Claiming a bound independent of drift (≤ σ^2) seems incorrect unless additional assumptions (e.g., zero-mean or vanishing drift) are made; this undermines the “maximize numerator alone” conclusion and the weight-independent constant C.
    定理 1 的 DSNR 代理界限似乎忽略了分母 Eε[||Δagg − Δ*||^2];当 Δi = Δ* + di + εi 且软最大权重加和为 1，该分母等于 ||∑我会死||^2 + Σε w_i^2 Var（εi），因此通常包含依赖权重的漂移贡献。声称存在与漂移无关的上界（≤ σ^2）似乎不正确，除非有额外假设（例如零均值漂移或零漂移）;这削弱了“仅分子最大化”的结论和权重无关常数 C。
  - The stated non-convex convergence rate in the abstract (O(1/√T)) conflicts with the theorem body, which shows a bound of the form O((F(x0)−F*)/(ηT) + σ^2 + ΓDAFA) with constant step size—i.e., asymptotic non-vanishing error. This inconsistency needs correction and a precise statement of the rate conditions (e.g., diminishing step sizes).
    抽象中所述的非凸收敛率（O（1/√T））与定理主体相冲突，定理主体显示了一个形如 O（（F（x0）−F*）/（ηT） + σ^2 + ΓDAFA）且步长恒定的界限——即渐近非零误差。这种不一致需要纠正，并精确说明速率条件（例如，步长递减）。
  - The claim of a “strictly improved heterogeneity constant” (ΓDAFA < ΓFedAvg) is asserted but not rigorously derived; ΓDAFA is defined as E[∑i wi ||di||^2], which is an upper bound on the squared drift of the weighted sum but not equal to it. Showing it is strictly smaller than the uniform case requires assumptions on correlation between alignment and drift magnitudes that are not formalized.
    声称存在“严格改进异质性常数”（ΓDAFA < ΓFedAvg），但未被严格推导;ΓDAFA 定义为 E[∑i wi ||Di||^2]，它是加权和平方漂移的上界，但不等于加权和。要证明它严格小于均匀情况，需要对比对与漂移大小之间的相关性假设，而这些假设并未被形式化。
  - Lipschitz claims for the softmax mapping used in Theorem 2 (bias bound) are imprecise; the softmax Jacobian’s operator norms and the propagation of perturbations from Δi to si = ⟨vi, v*⟩ require careful handling of normalization and directional noise, which is currently heuristic.
    Lipschitz 关于定理 2 中使用的软极大映射（偏置束缚）的主张不精确;软最大雅可比算子范数以及从Δi 到 si = ⟨vi， v*⟩的微扰传播需要对归一化和方向噪声进行严格处理，而这目前仍是启发式的。

- Experimental gaps or methodological issues

  
  实验空白或方法论问题

  - Missing important baselines: server-momentum and adaptive server optimizers (e.g., FedAvgM, FedAdam/FedOpt, FedYogi) are standard and could account for part of the observed gains; DAFA’s proxy relies on server momentum, so a comparison to strong FedAvgM/FedOpt baselines is essential.
    缺少重要基线：服务器动量和自适应服务器优化器（如 FedAvgM、FedAdam/FedOpt、FedYogi）是标准配置，可能解释部分观察到的收益;DAFA 的代理依赖服务器动量，因此与强劲的 FedAvgM/FedOpt 基线进行比较至关重要。
  - The proposed weighting discards dataset-size weighting (pi). In settings with highly imbalanced client data sizes (e.g., FEMNIST), fairness and statistical efficiency may be affected. A variant with pi·exp(γ si) and a comparison would clarify trade-offs.
    所提加权排除了数据集大小加权（pi）。在客户数据高度不平衡的环境中（如 FEMNIST），公平性和统计效率可能会受到影响。如果有一个带有 pi·exp（γ si） 的变体并进行比较，可以明确权衡。
  - DSNR measurement uses a centralized validation subset, which is often infeasible in privacy-preserving FL; this makes the DSNR figures less generalizable. Alternatives or sensitivity to proxy quality without centralized data are not explored.
    DSNR 测量使用集中验证子集，这在保护隐私的 FL 中通常不可行;这使得 DSNR 数据的推广性降低。未探讨替代方案或对无中心化数据代理质量敏感性的判断。
  - Hyperparameter tuning fairness across baselines is unclear; methods like SCAFFOLD and FedProx can be sensitive to LR and local steps and may require separate tuning for a fair comparison.
    超参数调优在基线间的公平性尚不明确;像 SCAFFOLD 和 FedProx 这样的方法对 LR 和局部步长敏感，可能需要单独调优以实现公平比较。

- Clarity or presentation issues

  
  清晰度或呈现问题

  - Some minor inconsistencies/typos (e.g., table labels “Dir(a = 0.1”, parenthesis mismatches) and missing mention in the algorithm of the practical safeguard for small update norms that is used in experiments.
    一些小的不一致/错别字（例如表标签“Dir（a = 0.1”），括号不匹配，以及算法中未提及实验中使用的小更新规范实际保障措施。
  - The relationship between the empirical DSNR proxy and the theoretical DSNR (based on vtrue) could be clarified further, including when the proxy is inaccurate.
    经验 DSNR 代理与理论 DSNR（基于 vtrue）之间的关系可以进一步澄清，包括代理不准确时。

- Missing related work or comparisons

  
  缺少相关工作或比较

  - Limited coverage of server-side optimizer literature (FedOpt family), server momentum (FedAvgM), and robust aggregation (median/trimmed-mean/Krum), which are relevant both practically and for claims about stability and robustness.
    对服务器端优化器文献（FedOpt 家族）、服务器动量（FedAvgM）和稳健聚合（中位数/修剪均值/Krum）的报道有限，这些内容在实际应用中及稳定性和鲁棒性的主张中都相关。
  - There exist prior works that consider cosine-similarity-based aggregation or directional reweighting in FL; these should be explicitly cited and contrasted to establish novelty beyond the DSNR framing.
    已有研究考虑基于余弦相似度的聚合或方向重权重在佛罗里达中;这些内容应被明确引用并对比，以确立其超越 DSNR 框架的新颖性。



### Detailed Comments 详细评论

- Technical soundness evaluation

  
  技术可靠性评估

  - The DSNR formulation is insightful, but the surrogate bound in Theorem 1 appears flawed due to omission of drift in the denominator expectation (which is only over ε). Because ||∑i wi di||^2 depends on the weights, the proposed lower bound with a weight-independent denominator constant C is not justified as written. A corrected bound would likely retain a denominator term involving δ and w, complicating the maximization argument and weakening the claim that maximizing ∑i wi⟨vi, v*⟩ suffices.
    DSNR 表述很有见地，但定理 1 中替代界限似乎存在缺陷，因为分母期望中缺少漂移（仅在ε上）。因为||∑我会死||^2 取决于权重，提出的权重无关分母常数 C 的下界并不符合书面描述。修正后的界限很可能会保留包含δ和 w 的分母项，这使最大化论证变得复杂，也削弱了最大化∑i wi⟨vi， v*⟩的主张。
  - The bias bound (Theorem 2) relies on a softmax Lipschitz property in ℓ1 with constant O(γ) and on bounding changes in scores si due to di, εi. This requires careful handling of the normalization vi = Δi/||Δi|| and the induced nonlinearity; a rigorous derivation with explicit constants and conditions (e.g., lower bound μ on magnitudes) would strengthen the result. As written, it reads more as a heuristic sketch.
    偏置界限（定理 2）依赖于 l1 中带有常数 O（γ）的软极大利普希茨性质，以及因 di、εi 导致分数 si 的边界变化。这需要仔细处理归一化 vi = Δi/||Δi||以及诱导非线性;带有明确常数和条件（例如对大小的下界μ）进行严格推导将增强结果。按原文写法，它更像是一则启发式草图。
  - The convergence result’s rate statement needs correction; if a constant stepsize is used, the asymptotic error floor remains, so the O(1/√T) claim in the abstract should be aligned with the theorem (e.g., by specifying diminishing stepsizes or by providing the precise rate).
    收敛结果的速率声明需要修正;如果使用恒定步长，渐近误差下限依然存在，因此抽象中的 O（1/√T）请求应与定理对齐（例如指定递减步长或提供精确速率）。
  - The “improved heterogeneity constant” claim is intuitively plausible under Assumption 5 (proxy positively aligned with vtrue), but a rigorous inequality ΓDAFA < ΓFedAvg requires either assuming a relationship between ||di|| and alignment with v* or proving that weighting by exp(γ⟨vi, v*⟩) reduces E||∑i wi di||^2. Presently, this is argued at a high level.
    “改进异质常数”这一主张在假设 5（代理与 vtrue 正对齐）下直观上是合理的，但严格的不等式ΓDAFA<ΓFedAvg需要假设||之间存在关系。Di||并且与 v* 的比对或证明通过 exp（γ⟨vi， v*⟩） 加权会减少 E||∑我会死||^2.目前，这一观点在高层次上被争论。

- Experimental evaluation assessment

  
  实验评估评估

  - The breadth of datasets and inclusion of typical FL baselines are positives. The ablations on γ and β convincingly show the bias-variance trade-off and the benefit of temporal smoothing.
    数据集的广泛性和典型 FL 基线的纳入是积极的。γ和β的消融过程令人信服地展示了偏差与方差的权衡以及时间平滑的好处。
  - However, omission of FedAvgM and FedOpt family is a significant gap, especially given DAFA uses a momentum proxy; part of the observed stability might be attributed to server momentum itself. Adding these baselines (and possibly Q-FFL/FedDyn/FedAdam) would better isolate the directional weighting’s incremental value.
    然而，遗漏 FedAvgM 和 FedOpt 家族是一个显著的差距，尤其是考虑到 DAFA 使用动量代理;观察到的稳定性部分可能归因于服务器动量本身。加入这些基线（以及可能的 Q-FFL/FedDyn/FedAdam）可以更好地隔离方向加权的增量值。
  - Using identical local LRs and K across baselines improves isolation of aggregation effects, but some methods (SCAFFOLD, FedProx) benefit from tuned hyperparameters; reporting sensitivity or tuned baselines would strengthen the fairness.
    在基线间使用相同的局部 LR 和 K 可以提升聚合效应的隔离，但某些方法（如 SCAFFOLD、FedProx）更受益于调优超参数;报告敏感度或调整基线将增强公平性。
  - DSNR evaluation with a centralized validation subset is informative but not always practical; presenting proxy-only metrics (e.g., ∑i wi⟨vi, v*⟩ over time) and/or decentralized surrogates would improve applicability.
    采用集中验证子集进行 DSNR 评估具有信息量，但并不总是实用;呈现仅代理指标（例如∑I WI⟨vi， v*⟩随时间变化）和/或去中心化代理将提升适用性。
  - It would be informative to see results under client data size imbalance and to compare pure alignment weighting to pi·exp(γ si) weighting.
    如果能看到在客户数据量不平衡下的结果，并将纯比对加权与 pi·exp（γ si）加权进行比较，将会很有帮助。

- Comparison with related work

  
  与相关研究的比较

  - The related work section correctly situates FedAvg/FedProx/SCAFFOLD/FedNova and notes connections to gradient conflict literature (PCGrad, CAGrad). However, the omission of server-optimizer work (FedOpt: FedAdam/FedYogi/FedAdagrad), server momentum (FedAvgM), and prior similarity-based or clustering-based aggregation methods weakens the novelty claim. Explicitly contrasting DAFA’s directional weighting-and-proxy mechanism and DSNR-based motivation with those works is needed.
    相关工作部分正确定位了 FedAvg/FedProx/SCAFFOLD/FedNova，并指出了与梯度冲突文献（PCGrad、CAGrad）的联系。然而，缺少服务器优化器工作（FedOpt：FedAdam/FedYogi/FedAdagrad）、服务器动量（FedAvgM）以及之前基于相似性或聚类的聚合方法，削弱了新颖性主张。需要明确对比 DAFA 的方向加权与代理机制以及基于 DSNR 的动机与这些研究。
  - Robust aggregation methods (median/trimmed-mean/Krum) are relevant to the stated limitation about adversarial/misaligned clients; discussing potential integrations would be constructive.
    稳健聚合方法（中位数/修剪均值/克鲁姆）与对抗性/错位客户端的限制相关;讨论潜在的整合会很有建设性。

- Discussion of broader impact and significance

  
  更广泛影响与意义的讨论

  - If validated, DAFA’s simplicity and performance gains make it attractive for practical FL deployments. The directional framing (DSNR) is a useful conceptual tool that could influence future designs in robust and personalized FL.
    如果经过验证，DAFA 的简洁性和性能提升使其在实际 FL 部署中具有吸引力。定向定向框架（DSNR）是一种有用的概念工具，可能影响未来稳健且个性化 FL 的设计。
  - Risks include susceptibility to adversarial clients that can align with the proxy to gain influence; integrating robustness is a necessary next step. The need for a centralized validation set to evaluate DSNR (even if only for analysis) can contradict strict privacy constraints; offering privacy-preserving or decentralized diagnostics would broaden applicability.
    风险包括容易受到可能与代理人结盟以获得影响力的对抗客户;整合稳健性是必要的下一步。需要集中式验证集来评估 DSNR（即使仅用于分析）可能与严格的隐私约束相矛盾;提供保护隐私或去中心化的诊断工具将扩大适用范围。



### Questions for Authors 作者提问

1. Theorem 1: How do you justify Eε[||Δagg − Δ*||^2] ≤ σ^2 without accounting for the deterministic drift term ||∑i wi di||^2, which depends on w? Can you provide a corrected DSNR lower bound that includes drift and clarify whether the bound remains useful for guiding weight selection?
   定理 1：如何证明 Eε[||Δagg − Δ*||^2] ≤ σ^2 未考虑确定性漂移项||∑我会死||^2，这取决于 w？你能提供一个包含漂移的修正 DSNR 下界，并澄清该界限是否仍适用于引导权重选择吗？
2. Convergence rate: The abstract states O(1/√T) non-convex convergence, but Theorem 3 shows a bound with a non-vanishing floor under constant stepsize. Under what stepsize schedule do you obtain O(1/√T) and can you restate the theorem precisely to match the claimed rate?
   收敛率：摘要中指出 O（1/√T）非凸收敛，但定理 3 显示在恒定步长下，下限为非零的底。你在什么步长表下得到 O（1/√T），你能精确地重述定理以匹配所宣称的速率吗？
3. Heterogeneity constant: What assumptions are needed to rigorously show ΓDAFA < ΓFedAvg beyond proxy reliability (e.g., correlation between alignment with v* and smaller drift norms)? Can you provide a formal proof or a general bound (beyond the two-cluster toy model)?
   异质性常数：除了代理可靠性外，严格证明ΓDAFA<ΓFedAvg（例如与v*的比对与较小漂移范数之间的相关性）需要哪些假设？你能提供一个形式上的证明或一般界限（超越双簇玩具模型）吗？
4. Baselines: Could you add comparisons to FedAvgM and FedOpt (FedAdam/FedYogi) to disentangle the effect of server momentum from your alignment weighting? Since DAFA relies on a momentum proxy, these are arguably the most relevant baselines.
   基线：你能不能加上 FedAvgM 和 FedOpt（FedAdam/FedYogi）的对比，来区分服务器动量与阵营权重的影响？由于 DAFA 依赖于动量代理，这些可以说是最相关的基线。
5. Weighting by data size: Have you tried w_i ∝ p_i·exp(γ si)? How does it compare to pure alignment weighting, especially on datasets with highly imbalanced client sizes (e.g., FEMNIST)?
   按数据量加权：你试过 w_i ∝ p_i·exp（γ si）吗？它与纯比对加权相比如何，尤其是在客户规模高度不平衡的数据集（如 FEMNIST）上？
6. Bias bound (Theorem 2): Can you formalize the Lipschitz constant of the softmax map with respect to the alignment scores and provide a rigorous derivation that accounts for normalization noise (vi = Δi/||Δi||) and the μ lower bound?
   偏置界限（定理 2）：你能否形式化软极大映射的利普希茨常数相对于比对分数，并提供一个严格的推导，考虑归一化噪声（vi = Δi/||Δi||）μ下界呢？
7. Practical safeguard: The experiments mention thresholding small ||Δi|| and setting si = 0. Could you incorporate this into the algorithm description and analyze its effect on the theoretical guarantees?
   实际保障：实验提到阈值小||Δi||并将 si = 0 设为 si。你能否将这些纳入算法描述，分析它对理论保证的影响？
8. DSNR evaluation: In the absence of any centralized data, what diagnostics would you recommend to tune γ and assess proxy reliability? Can you report results for such decentralized surrogates?
   DSNR 评估：在缺乏集中数据的情况下，您会推荐哪些诊断方法来调整γ并评估代理可靠性？你能报告这些去中心化替代品的结果吗？



### Overall Assessment 总体评估

This paper presents an intuitive and practical approach to mitigating directional disagreement in federated learning via softmax weighting by alignment with a momentum-based proxy, grounded in a DSNR perspective. The empirical results are promising across multiple benchmarks, with informative ablations and stability analyses that support the central intuition. However, key theoretical components need correction or strengthening: the DSNR surrogate lower bound appears to omit drift in the denominator; the convergence rate statement conflicts with the provided theorem; and the “improved heterogeneity constant” claim is argued heuristically rather than proven under clear assumptions. Experimentally, missing strong server-side optimizer baselines (FedAvgM/FedOpt) and lack of evaluation under data-size imbalance and privacy-compliant DSNR diagnostics are notable gaps. Given the potential impact and simplicity of DAFA, I view the contribution as valuable, but the paper in its current form falls short of NeurIPS standards for rigor and comprehensiveness. With corrected theory, stronger baselines, and expanded analysis of weighting by data size and decentralized diagnostics, this work could become a strong submission. My recommendation is borderline reject, primarily due to theoretical issues and missing baselines, while acknowledging the promising empirical signal and practical appeal.
本文提出了一种直观且实用的方法，通过与基于动量的代理对齐软最大权重来缓解联邦学习中的方向性分歧，并基于 DSNR 视角。多项基准测试的实证结果令人鼓舞，且有信息丰富的消融和稳定性分析支持了核心直觉。然而，关键的理论成分需要修正或加强：DSNR 的替代下界似乎省略了分母中的漂移;收敛率陈述与上述定理相冲突;而“改进异质常数”主张则是启发式论证，而非在明确假设下证明。实验上，缺少强有力的服务器端优化基线（FedAvgM/FedOpt）以及在数据量不平衡和隐私合规 DSNR 诊断下缺乏评估，是显著的缺口。鉴于 DAFA 的潜在影响力和简洁性，我认为其贡献有价值，但目前形式的论文在严谨性和全面性方面仍未达到 NeurIPS 的标准。随着理论修正、基线更强化，以及数据量和去中心化诊断加权分析的扩展，这项工作有望成为一份强有力的投稿。我的建议几乎是拒绝，主要原因是理论问题和缺乏基线，同时也承认其有前景的实证信号和实用价值。
